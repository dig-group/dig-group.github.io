Personal Profile

    I graduated with a PhD from Nanyang Technological University in Singapore under the supervision of S- K. Professor Lam and T Professor Srikanthan continued his postdoctoral work at Nanyang Technological University in Singapore. Joined Ocean University of China in December 2022 and received funding from national level postdoctoral overseas talent introduction programs and Shandong Province Bo Xin Program. Led the Central University Basic Research Special Fund and participated in general projects funded by the National Natural Science Foundation of China. My main research directions are data mining, deep learning, traffic big data analysis and application, software code security, etc. Published over ten papers in journals of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and CCF Class A conferences, including IEEE Transactions on Intelligent Transportation Systems, Information Sciences, IJCAI, SDM, Automation in Construction, etc. Served as a reviewer for multiple SCI journals in Zone 1. Welcome graduate and undergraduate students interested in the above research fields to join our team. Our team creates an excellent academic atmosphere and international exchange opportunities for student development. We maintain close cooperative relationships with multiple high-level research teams both domestically and internationally, and can annually promote multiple outstanding students to renowned foreign research institutions such as Nanyang Technological University in Singapore for six months or a year of exchange and visiting learning.
